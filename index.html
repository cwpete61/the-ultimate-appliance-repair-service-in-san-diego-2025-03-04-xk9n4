About SD Appliance Repair

Founded in 2003, SD Appliance Repair began with a simple mission: to provide San Diego residents with honest, reliable, and professional appliance repair services. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted appliance repair companies in Southern California, serving over 10,000 satisfied customers across the county.

Our core values of integrity, excellence, and customer satisfaction drive everything we do. We believe in transparent pricing, quality workmanship, and standing behind our repairs with industry-leading warranties. Our team of certified technicians undergoes continuous training to stay current with the latest appliance technologies and repair techniques.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of appliances do you repair?

We service all major household appliances including refrigerators, washers, dryers, dishwashers, ovens, ranges, microwaves, and more. Our technicians are certified to work on all major brands and models.

How quickly can you respond to service calls?

We offer same-day service for most repairs, with emergency service available 24/7. Most appointments can be scheduled within a 2-hour window, and we'll call when we're on the way.

What are your service rates and warranties?

Our service calls start at $85, which is applied to the repair cost. We provide upfront pricing before any work begins and offer a 90-day warranty on parts and labor for all repairs.

Do you offer commercial appliance repair?

Yes, we service commercial appliances for restaurants, hotels, and other businesses. Our technicians are certified for both residential and commercial equipment repairs.

What areas do you service in San Diego?

We serve all of San Diego County, including La Jolla, Del Mar, Carlsbad, Encinitas, Chula Vista, El Cajon, and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm service in your location.
